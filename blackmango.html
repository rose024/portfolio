About Black Mango

An online clothing store selling staple wardrobe pieces and althesure clothing. Our goals at Black Mango is for our users to have a one to shop to find all things comfort and style. Having an easy to follow work flow and capabilities that allow users to have all needs met in one place.


The Process

For the duration of one and a half month, the team planned to make a mockup for an online clothing store. From the name itself Black Mango, we made our colour palette into black and yellow (inspired from the colour of mango). Users are able to register their account, view the collection and filter, order online, and view their profile.


Logo


Our team member, Kevin made the logo. He made an illustration of a mango coloured in black using Illustrator.


Design Process


As the UI/UX Designer, I designed some of the pages. We first made a low fidelity then after a couple of changes we made the high fidelity with prototype. The pages I made are the Login/Sign up, Home Page, Collection Page where users are able to view the clothes we sell, and Profile Page where users are able to sign out, check their previous orders and more.

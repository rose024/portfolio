About TeamVan

TeamVan is where I have done my co-op for my final term. One of my tasks was to re-design their website. This is a duration that spanned in three weeks. TeamVan Outsourcing is located on West Vancouver and is a company that helps other businesses by providing call center services in multiple languages and managing various operations efficiently.

Brainstorming

This is a solo project, with the COO giving and helping out with the guidelines and for any changes needed to be done along the way. I have used the platform GoHighLevel to build the website, I also did more research about the company TeamVan to be able to know more what I will plan to do. I made sure to make a progress report to the host at the end of the day. I also used Notion to easily track my progress and for the host to see the list/tasks I have done.

UI/UX Design Process

I collected first all the necessary files such as a copy of the logo. I also made a moodboard which consists of modern, ethical, and minimalistic. The logo did not need any changes but I did change the colour theme for consistency, I decided to make an inspiration from the old website and make a new colour theme from it.

Mood Board

Development Process


With the role of a Web Developer, I started out with the index page. During the process, I have faced trials and errors however it is all worth it as I liked the end process and so does my host/clients. The redesign focused on enhancing the user interface and to make it easier for users or their clients to explore and access the business's list of services. I decided to make it minimalistic and modern as this is a professional business, ensuring that the users will be able to easily navigate throughout the pages and book by adding a booking system.

I also was assigned to make a funnel website, I made sure to add a pop up for the funnel page and a call to action button that leads to the main website. I made a step-by-step pages to help them buy a service for a discounted price.

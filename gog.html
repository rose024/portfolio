Gift of Gratitude

Role

Web Developer, UI/UX Designer


Duration

3 weeks


Project Overview

GOG is a charity that is hosted by a group of friends who joined together to make the people in Vancouver's Christmas memorable and joyful. They sell donuts during Winter season and all profits they will be using it to give and gift on Christmas. They usually giveaway foods like donuts, clothings, and more. This event is personally hosted by one of my COO's and I asked if I can develop the website for them to be able to gather more volunteer and donations. This website will be using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

01 Brainstorming

Throughout three weeks, as a developer and designer. I listed out a sitemap and moodboard first to show it to the client and give them an initial idea what the website will look like. I also have done research and list out things needed in the website. Firstly, I made the wireframes so we can make changes before the development process. Then after, I started out developing the website.

Mood Board

02 Design Process

There already is a logo therefore I do not need to design for one but I made an inspiration from the colour of the logo they made to make a colour theme and typography. The logo they made is vibrant and bubbly which then I decided to make a sitemap and moodboard based from it, something that will make the website look fun by the colours and where the user will feel comfort.

Wireframe

03 Development Process


I focused on building a responsive, user-friendly platform using HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript. I planned to make the users be clarified easily about what the website is about and make the donation process as smooth as possible. One of the challenges I have encountered was implementing a "Donate" button, as it required both ease of use and flexibility for the charity. After exploring various options, I decided to use Squarespace and a Google Form to offer a simple and secure way for users to make donations or join as a volunteer.

Aside from errors, donation process was another challenge as the client did not provide the necessary information (banking details for donation) before the deadline. Despite this, I focused on creating a solid structure and design for the website, ensuring that donation could be easily added later without affecting the overall layout and user experience.

Project Insight

This website helped my experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and being able to connect and integrate with social media for latest news, JotForm for volunteer applications, and Squarespace for donations.

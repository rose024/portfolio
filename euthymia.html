About Euthymia

Euthymia is an app for people dealing with mental health problems/disorders. We will help the users improve their mental health condition and break bad habits. When opening the app the first time, it will let you take an assessment to test what type of problem you're dealing with to let you know what you're experiencing. Euthymia also helps you connect with a therapist with a cheaper option. Also, be able to learn more about mental health problems.


The Process

For the duration of three months, I made a mobile app mock-up. I plan to make the app where it offers exercise, activities, and mini-games that can help improve mental health. It includes also where you can talk to a therapist, they can be able to diagnose you, counselling, and do sessions.


Logo & Style Guide


I used a stock photo from Adobe Stock for the logo and added the name of the app. I incorporated the colour as green as it symbolizes peace, calm, and tranquility and it is the same color for mental health awareness. Just like the name Euthymia meaning calm and tranquility.

Project Overview

Black Mango is an online clothing store selling staple wardrobe pieces and althesure clothing. Our focus is not just the platform but also the methodology we plan to use which is the Waterfall methodology.

01 Goal

Throughout 6 weeks, the team planned to create an e-commerce application with a mockup. Our goals at Black Mango is for our users to shop to find all things for comfort and style, having an easy to follow work flow and capabilities that allow users to have all needs met in one place. Users are able to register their account, view the collection, filter their search, order online, and view their profile.

We made a site map to plan out what will the priorities be for our product. Then we listed out our tasks/work by also estimating the time we will be able to finish it up.


Waterfall Methodology

Since roles and tasks are well-defined and distributed to us based on our skills, Waterfall makes it easier for us to work together and complete tasks efficiently. We used a Gantt chart based on the list we made on our WBS. However, we underestimated our time and decided to make a second chart for our changes.

02 Design Process

From the name itself Black Mango, we made our colour palette into black, from the name itself and yellow, inspired from the colour of mango. We made a mood board to take a quick view of what our website will be inspired as.


Prototype


We decided to make a low fidelity wireframe first before moving on to creating a high fidelity wireframe and mock up of the website.

03 Development

Although we made only mockups for our app pages, we developed a page for an email confirmation using HTML, and CSS. I collaborated this process with another developer therefore we used Repl.it, a space where we can code together in one platform. This will be an email for a confirmation order that is web responsive where it contains the user's list of order with total value, the recipient's billing and mailing address.

Project Insights

Staying on top of tasks was critical due to the fact that some tasks couldn't get completed without specific deliverables. However, this project enhanced my experience through a lot of things from developing a website newsletter, to learning new strategies and to finalize our product and present our pitch.

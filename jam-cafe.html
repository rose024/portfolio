Play Expo

Role

UX/UI Designer, Graphic Designer


Duration

4 weeks


Project Overview

The project is designed as a pixelated, retro-style website to bring a space for gamers and game developers for a video game showcases. Inspired by retro games, the site allows users to find nearby game events, purchase tickets, and provides a platform for indie and established developers to showcase their work.

01 Goal

The goal was to capture the spirit of retro gaming while ensuring a smooth and intuitive user experience. I wanted to create a platform that not only feels like a tribute to indie video games but also brings people together in real life to celebrate gaming where they can be able to connect and make friends. The focus was on building an experience thatâ€™s both visually nostalgic and functionally modern, making it easy for users to explore events, buy tickets, and participate in the gaming community.

02 Design Process

Every design element, from the color palette to the pixelated icons, was crafted to create a nostalgic experience that gamers can immediately connect with. The logo was made with 6 designs to start ffrom sketch until I decided to design using Illustrator a controller with a crown at the top with a play button from the name 'Play Expo' itself.

Project Insights

By combining functionality with a nostalgic visual style, I created a platform that resonates deeply with the gaming community. This project gave me the chance to dive into pixel art and classic game-inspired design. I enjoyed using different element and plug-ins of Figma to create a pixelated interface.

What we do!

We are a group of Collaborators and volunteers united together to make the Holiday spirit better. Every Christmas on December 25, we would be touring around Vancouver and give out donuts from Krispy Kreme to give out to the community workers, sending out special notes and messages to the elderly homes, and more coming soon! Be able to join us by volunteering or donating, any amount will be appreciated.

What We Give

Donuts

We donate around 50+ boxes of Krispy Kreme donuts to people around Downtown, Vancouver such as the custodians, homeless, and more.

Sending Cards to the Elderly Homes

It does gets lonely at the Senior home during holidays, so we decided to help brighten up the mood by sending them positive messages with DIY cards and notes.

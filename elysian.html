About Elysian

Elysian is a cafe located in Vancouver. They offer sweet delicacies such as cake slices, beverages, and more. Users are able to loginand register to make a reservation.


The Process

For the duration of two weeks, I made a cafe that is web responsive. It can be able to view on mobile, desktop, and tablet. For the redesign, I worked with the team and we included PHP for users to be able to login.


Logo


I used a stock photo from Adobe Stock for the logo and added the name of the cafe. It is a slice of a cake indicating sweets and delicacy.


Development Process


Using jQuery and slick slider, I made a carousel/gallery to make them fully web responsive for any devices suchas mobile, or desktop. As I am both a Designer and Developer, I got the style guide for a blue green to make it look classy.

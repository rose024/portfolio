TeamVan

Role

Web Developer, UI/UX Designer


Duration

5 weeks


Project Overview

TeamVan is where I have done my co-op for my final term. One of my tasks was to re-design their website. This is a duration that spanned in three weeks. TeamVan Outsourcing is located on West Vancouver and is a company that helps other businesses by providing call center services in multiple languages and managing various operations efficiently.

01 Goal

I have used the platform GoHighLevel to build the website, I also did more research about the company TeamVan to be able to know more and what I will plan to do. I made sure to make a progress report to the host at the end of each day. I also used Notion to easily track my progress and for the host to see the list/tasks I have done.

02 Design Process

I collected all the necessary files such as a copy of the logo. I also made a moodboard that consists of modern, and minimalistic look. The logo was already provided, but I did change the colour palette for consistency, I decided to make an inspiration from the old website and make a new colour palette from it.

Mood Board

03 Development Process


The redesign focused on enhancing the look and user interface to make it easier for users or their clients to explore and access the business's list of services. I decided to make it minimalistic and modern as this is a professional business, ensuring that the users will be able to easily navigate throughout the pages and book an appointment by adding a booking system.

I also was assigned to make a funnel website, I made sure to add a pop up for the funnel page and a call to action button that leads to the main website. I made a step-by-step pages to help them buy a service for a discounted price.

Project Insights


Creating the website helped me gain a deep understanding in GoHighLevel platform and being able to explore more of its features such as plug-ins and integration. This was my first time using the said platform before I entered my inetrnship for TeamVan. GoHighLevel is quite beneficial and easy to use as they can also integrate their social media to be able to receive and respond messages from different social medias in one platform and connect with Google account for booking system.
